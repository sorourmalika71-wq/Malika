Step 1 – Define the Goal

Study plan progress, and engagement. Provide time management tips. Focus on simple learning patterns for students and teachers. Our goal is to provide every student with different strategies (techniques) that help study plan progress and engagement. By providing time management tips and focusing on simple learning patterns for students, this would help maintain a well-structured learning.

Step 2 – Define the target Audience

Students interested in studying digital marketing and want to track their learning.

Step 3 – Select Key Metrics

  • Completed tasks vs subjects
  • Time spent studying each subject
  • Actual time against planned

Step 4 – Organize the Data

  • Time periods (daily, weekly, monthly)
  • Tasks actual against finished
  • Quizzes actual against finished
  • Progress vs Goal

Step 5 – Visualize the Data

  • Line chart for the actual time against plan
  • Pie chart for the time spent studying each subject
